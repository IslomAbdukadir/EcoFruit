Raisin Sultana-Aftobi

Raisin

Dried raisins are obtained from grapes. Drying of grapes and production of raisins has a long history. Raisins are one of Iran's strategic products. fertile rural economy, high-quality agricultural land, the use of modern and organic technologies in the production of raisins have made it one of the largest exporters raisins, and now Iranian raisins take their rightful place in the world. We offer raisins in packages 250g, 400g, 1kg and 10kg each.

Sultana-Aftobi raisin is a natural product made from light grape varieties. This variety of raisins is presented by the company in two positions: the highest grade and the first. In the dried in the form of raisins are large berries of a golden or light brown color, about the same size. Here you can buy Sultana-Aftobi raisins in bulk for subsequent sale in their outlets or use in confectionery shops. Annual the volume of production of raisins is 10,000 tons. When ordering from 100 tons, the company will make a discount and flexible terms of payment. You can choose the packaging of the goods according to your requirement. Producing country - Uzbekistan, Samarkand. The minimum lot of the order is from 20 tons. In case of occurrence additional questions, please contact.
